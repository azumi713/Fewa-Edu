Fewa Kokusai Educational Consultancy Pvt. Ltd, is a premier education consultancy offering language courses, preparation, and university placement services in Japan. Registered and approved by the Government of Nepal, Fewa was founded with the mission to provide exceptional Japanese language education and consultancy services in 2017. Our experienced instructors and dedicated team are committed to helping students and professionals alike achieve fluency in Japanese and reach their academic and career goals in Japan.
